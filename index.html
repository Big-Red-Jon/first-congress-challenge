Jim Cooper: Congressman of the 5th District of Tennessee

Political Party: Democrat

The 5th Congressional District of Tennessee includes all of Davidson County and Dickson County, and part of Cheatham County. Over 700,000 people live in both rural and urban areas in the heart of Middle Tennessee.

Committee Assignments Include:

  • Committee on Armed Services
  • Committee on the Budget

Jim is current sponsoring many acts in congress, including the Redistricting Transparency Act of 2021, as well as the John Tanner Fairness and Independence in Redistricting Act. To learn more about Jim's sponsorships visit his legislative page here!
